App Notice

Logo

Use the the official “Made with App Game Kit” logo available for download from this Internet URL; www.appgamekit.com/media/made-with-appgamekit-logo.zip

The icon must be kept in the correct aspect ratio and not be reduced lower than 64x64 pixels.

The logo kit contains images with resolutions varying from 64x64 to 512x512.
