Adding an IAP for Apple

To add an in app purchase on iTunes Connect -

  • Login to iTunes Connect
  • Click on the product you want to edit
  • Select Features and then In-App Purchases
  • Click on the + button
  • Select either Consumable or Non-Consumable
  • Fill in the remainder of the form
  • The product ID is used as a way to identify the product e.g. com.mycompany.myracinggame.extralevelspack1.
