Adding an IAP for Google

To add an in app purchase on the Google Play Developer Console -

  • Login to the Google Play Developer Console
  • Click on the product you want to edit
  • Select In-app Products from the menu on the left
  • Click on Add new product
  • Select Managed product
  • Enter a product ID - this is used as a way to identify the product e.g. com.mycompany.myracinggame.extralevelspack1.
  • Continue to fill in the form providing details about pricing and availability
