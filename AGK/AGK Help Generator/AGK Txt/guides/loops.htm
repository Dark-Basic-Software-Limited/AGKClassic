Working with loops

Beginner Level Tutorial

What you will learn

Loops play an important role in programming. They are used to repeat a certain series of instructions over and over. This guide will cover the basics of what loops are, introduce you to the different kinds of loops and also demonstrate some ways in which loops can be used.

  • Do loops
  • While
  • Repeat until
  • For
  • Drawing sprites with for loops
  • Drawing circles with for loops
  • Exiting loops
  • Continue in loops
  • Conclusion
