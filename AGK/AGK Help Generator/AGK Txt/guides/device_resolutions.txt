Device Resolutions

_sheader
iPhone / iPod

_text
iPhone, iPhone 3G, iPhone 3GS and earlier iPod Touch models all have a display resolution of 480 x 320.

_text
The iPhone 4 has a display resolution of 960 x 640. This is also the case for the 4th generation iPod Touch.

_sheader
iPad

_text
iPad 1 and iPad 2 have a display resolution of 1024 x 768. The iPad 3 and 4 have a resolution of 2048 x 1536 but it is recommended you still design for 1024 x 768 on these devices for memory reasons.

_sheader
Netbooks

_text
Netbooks typically have a resolution of 1024 x 600 or 1024 x 768.

_sheader
Android

_text
Android devices vary dramatically from 320 x 240 at the smallest to 1920 x 1080 at the high end. Typically widescreen tablets are 1280 x 800.
